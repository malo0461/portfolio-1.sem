Background

The brief for this projekt was to make a gamificated advertisement for Privat Snak by Sex & Samfund. The target group was 13-15 year olds.

Approach

This was our first time working with the SCRUM-concept in a group. This new work methodology consisted of new procedures such as daily scrum, keeping af scrumboard and updating a burndown chart.

We were also introduced to the use of Laswell's model of communication and the making of personas as representatives for the target group.

Process

We conducted a think-aloud protocol on a person in the target group.

Our process was uploaded to a documentation website.

At the vernissage, we conducted a BERT test on the guests to evaluate our product, the result of which can be seen on the documentation website.

The animation was drawn in Adobe Illustrator, and was coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript using Brackets.
