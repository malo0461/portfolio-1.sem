Background

The brief for this projekt was to make an app for the office community Republikken and its customers.

Approach

We followed the SCRUM methodology for group work, and used the business model canvas by means of illuminating Republikken's company strategy and relationship with customers.

Process

Republikken had conducted a member survey, which gave us material for data mining. After brainstorming, making paper prototypes and benchmarking, the choice settled on R Connect.

Our process was uploaded to a documentation website. We coded a presentation website for the pitch to the customer (Republikken).

Our app layout was designed in Adobe Photoshop, and imported to XD to make a digital prototype.

The websites were coded in HTML and CSS using Brackets.

Reflection

I was involved only in the last half of this project; if this hadn't been the case, I would have liked to evovle the concept into more of a visual sharing platform, and also to bring a more minimalist approach to the design.
