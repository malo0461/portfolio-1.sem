Background

The brief for this projekt was to make a site about a historical art movement directed at 13 to 16 year olds. My subject was street art.

Approach

First step was to make the content for the site. It had to appeal to the target group while being informative. I researched relevant text and pictures, and selected artists for exemplification based also on my own knowledge of the scene, so that it would have a contemporary touch.

Process

Next came designing the visual expression of the site. It should be responsive to the most common screen sizes, and so needed splash images in corresponding sizes. I also designed an icon to fit the subject and convey the idea visually. Last step before implementing was to make a styletile as guide for coding the website. My design process can be found here.

The site was coded in HTML and CSS using Brackets.
