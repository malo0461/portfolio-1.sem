Background

The brief for this projekt was to produce a video for Everyday Project under CPH:DOX. The theme had to be either 'self-absorption' or 'democracy', and the video should tell an extraordinary story about a seemingly ordinary person.

Approach

We came into contact with artist Johanne Ã˜stervang. Among many things, she produces videos for youtube with herself as the subject. Thus, our video would fall under the theme 'self-absorption', but would portray it from her artistic point of view.

Process

Our preparation consisted of creating a synopsis with an interview guide and a storyboard for the video. We met Johanne at her studio at Charlottenborg. After filming we went to her office for the interview.

The video was shot with two Canon DSLR cameras; the two microphones on set both failed us, which resulted in our only editable sound being from one of the cameras.

The sound was edited in Adobe Audition, and the video in PremierePro.
